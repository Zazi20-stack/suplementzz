Ashwagandha Root Extract (KSM-66)600 mg
Cortisol modulation & testosterone support
Cortisolmodulatie & testosteronondersteuning

KSM-66 is a full-spectrum ashwagandha extract standardised to ≥5% withanolides. Chronic stress elevates cortisol, which directly suppresses GnRH and LH — reducing testicular testosterone synthesis. Ashwagandha's withanolides attenuate the HPA axis stress response, lowering cortisol and allowing the HPG axis to function more normally. Multiple RCTs confirm significant testosterone elevation in stressed and subclinically hypogonadal men.

KSM-66 is een volledig-spectrum ashwagandha-extract gestandaardiseerd op ≥5% withanoliden. Chronische stress verhoogt cortisol, wat GnRH en LH direct onderdrukt. De withanoliden van ashwagandha dempen de HPA-assrespons, waardoor de HPG-as normaler kan functioneren.

Free testosterone support
Vrij testosteron ondersteuning

Fenugreek furostanolic saponins inhibit aromatase and 5α-reductase — reducing testosterone conversion to oestradiol and DHT. Standardised extracts at 500 mg/day have demonstrated significant improvements in free testosterone, libido, and strength in double-blind RCTs in healthy men aged 25–52.

Furostanolische saponinen in fenegriek remmen aromatase en 5α-reductase, waardoor testosteron minder wordt omgezet naar oestradiol en DHT. RCT's tonen significante verbeteringen in vrij testosteron en libido.

LH signalling & aromatase inhibition

Zinc is essential for LH receptor sensitivity in Leydig cells and functions as a natural aromatase inhibitor. Marginal zinc deficiency is associated with measurably lower testosterone. The HPA–HPG axis connection
De HPA–HPG-asverbinding
1

Chronic stress: Sustained stress activates the HPA axis, elevating cortisol. Cortisol inhibits GnRH release from the hypothalamus — suppressing the entire downstream hormonal cascade.

Chronische stress: Aanhoudende stress activeert de HPA-as en verhoogt cortisol. Cortisol remt de afgifte van GnRH vanuit de hypothalamus — waardoor de volledige downstream hormonale cascade wordt onderdrukt.

2

Downstream suppression: Reduced GnRH leads to lower LH and FSH. LH is the primary signal for Leydig cells to synthesise testosterone — so chronically elevated cortisol functionally lowers testosterone even in healthy men.

Downstream onderdrukking: Verminderd GnRH leidt tot lagere LH- en FSH-waarden. LH is het primaire signaal voor Leydig-cellen om testosteron te synthetiseren — chronisch verhoogd cortisol verlaagt dus functioneel testosteron.

3

Our approach: Ashwagandha attenuates cortisol. Zinc and Vitamin D3 support Leydig cell function directly. Fenugreek reduces testosterone conversion. Magnesium increases free testosterone via SHBG competition. Together they address the axis at multiple levels.

Onze aanpak: Ashwagandha dempt cortisol. Zink en vitamine D3 ondersteunen direct de Leydig-celfunctie. Fenegriek vermindert testosteronconversie. Magnesium verhoogt vrij testosteron via SHBG-competitie.

01
Examining the effect of Withania somnifera supplementation on muscle strength and recovery
Effect van Withania somnifera-suppletie op spierkracht en herstel
Journal of the International Society of Sports Nutrition · Wankhede et al., 2015 · RCT, n=57

Men receiving 300 mg KSM-66 ashwagandha twice daily for 8 weeks showed significantly greater increases in testosterone (p=0.04), muscle strength, and recovery compared to placebo. Serum cortisol was also significantly lower in the treatment group.

Mannen die 8 weken lang tweemaal daags 300 mg KSM-66 ashwagandha ontvingen, toonden significant grotere stijgingen in testosteron (p=0,04), spierkracht en herstel vergeleken met placebo.

Limitation: Relatively small sample (n=57). Conducted in resistance-trained men — results may not fully generalise to sedentary populations.

Beperking: Relatief kleine steekproef (n=57). Uitgevoerd bij krachtgetrainde mannen — resultaten zijn mogelijk niet volledig generaliseerbaar naar sedentaire populaties.

02
Effect of fenugreek extract supplementation on testosterone levels in healthy men
Effect van fenegriekextractsuppletie op testosteronniveaus bij gezonde mannen
Phytotherapy Research · Steels et al., 2011 · RCT, n=60

Healthy men (25–52 years) receiving 600 mg/day standardised fenugreek extract for 6 weeks demonstrated significant improvements in free testosterone levels, sexual function scores, and anabolic activity compared to placebo.

Gezonde mannen (25–52 jaar) die 6 weken lang 600 mg/dag gestandaardiseerd fenegriekextract kregen, vertoonden significante verbeteringen in vrij testosteron, seksuele functie en anabole activiteit vergeleken met placebo.

Limitation: 6-week duration is relatively short. Funded in part by the manufacturer of the patented extract — potential for bias.

Beperking: 6 weken is relatief kort. Deels gefinancierd door de fabrikant van het gepatenteerde extract — potentieel voor bias.

RCT
03
Vitamin D supplementation and testosterone in men: a 12-month RCT
Vitamine D-suppletie en testosteron bij mannen: een 12-maanden RCT
Hormone and Metabolic Research · Pilz et al., 2011 · RCT, n=54

Men supplemented with 3,332 IU vitamin D3 daily for 12 months showed significant increases in total testosterone (+25.2%), free testosterone (+20.3%), and bioactive testosterone (+18.3%) compared to placebo. Baseline 25(OH)D in the treatment group was in the insufficient range.

Mannen die 12 maanden lang 3.332 IE vitamine D3 dagelijks kregen, vertoonden significante stijgingen in totaal testosteron (+25,2%), vrij testosteron (+20,3%) en bioactief testosteron (+18,3%) vergeleken met placebo.

Limitation: Small sample (n=54). Benefit may be attenuated in men who are already vitamin D sufficient.

Beperking: Kleine steekproef (n=54). Voordeel kan kleiner zijn bij mannen die al vitamine D-voldoende zijn.
