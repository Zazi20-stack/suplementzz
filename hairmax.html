Biotin (Vitamin B7)5,000 mcg
Keratin synthesis
Keratinesynthese

Biotin acts as a coenzyme for carboxylase enzymes involved in fatty acid synthesis and amino acid metabolism — both essential for keratin production. Clinical evidence supports doses of 2,500–5,000 mcg for hair outcomes; higher doses exceed what the evidence supports and are excreted as waste.

Biotine werkt als co-enzym voor carboxylase-enzymen betrokken bij vetzuursynthese en aminozuurmetabolisme — beide essentieel voor keratineproductie. Klinisch bewijs ondersteunt doses van 2.500–5.000 mcg voor haaruitkomsten.

Saw Palmetto Extract320 mg
5α-reductase inhibition
5α-reductaseremming

Serenoa repens inhibits both isoforms of 5α-reductase (type I and II), reducing local conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT). DHT binds androgen receptors in scalp follicles, progressively miniaturising them. At 320 mg/day of a standardised liposterolic extract, inhibition is clinically meaningful without the systemic side-effect profile of pharmaceutical alternatives.

Serenoa repens remt beide isovormen van 5α-reductase (type I en II), waardoor de lokale omzetting van testosteron naar dihydrotestosteron (DHT) wordt verminderd. DHT bindt androgeenreceptoren in hoofdhaarfollikels, waardoor ze geleidelijk miniaturiseren.

L-Cysteine500 mg
Disulphide bond formation
Disulfidebindingsvorming

Keratin is approximately 14–18% cysteine by amino acid composition. Disulphide bonds between cysteine residues give the hair shaft its tensile strength. Oral L-cysteine raises plasma cysteine, supporting keratin synthesis in actively dividing matrix cells at the follicle base — particularly relevant when dietary protein intake is suboptimal.

Keratine bestaat voor ongeveer 14–18% uit cysteïne. Disulfidebindingen geven de haarschacht zijn treksterkte. Oraal L-cysteïne verhoogt plasma-cysteïne, ondersteunt keratinesynthese in actief delende matrixcellen bij de basis van de follikel.

Zinc Picolinate15 mg
Follicle cell proliferation
Follikelcelproliferatie

Zinc is a cofactor for enzymes critical for rapid cell division in the hair matrix. Serum zinc deficiency is significantly more prevalent in patients with telogen effluvium and alopecia areata. Picolinate is chosen for superior bioavailability. Dose is set at 15 mg — within the therapeutic range and safely below the upper intake level of 25 mg/day.

Zink is een cofactor voor enzymen die essentieel zijn voor snelle celdeling in de haarmatrix. Serumzinktekort komt significant vaker voor bij patiënten met telogeen effluvium en alopecia areata.

Silica & antioxidant support
Silica & antioxidantondersteuning

Equisetum arvense is among the highest plant sources of bioavailable orthosilicic acid. Silicon is incorporated into glycosaminoglycans and collagen in connective tissue surrounding the dermal papilla, associated with improved hair tensile strength in pilot trials. Also contains kaempferol and quercetin — flavonoids with antioxidant activity at follicle level.

Equisetum arvense behoort tot de hoogste plantaardige bronnen van biologisch beschikbaar orthosiliciumzuur. Silicium wordt ingebouwd in glycosaminoglycanen en collageen rondom de dermale papil.

Vitamin D3 (Cholecalciferol)25 mcg (1,000 IU)
Follicle cycle regulation
Follikelcyclusregulatie

Vitamin D receptors (VDR) are expressed in keratinocytes and dermal papilla cells throughout the hair cycle. VDR knockout mouse models show profound hair loss, establishing a causal role. Serum 25(OH)D below 20 ng/mL is consistently associated with non-scarring alopecias. 1,000 IU targets sufficiency in most adults without approaching toxicity thresholds (>4,000 IU/day chronically).

Vitamine D-receptoren (VDR) worden tot expressie gebracht in keratinocyten en dermale papilcellen. VDR-knockout muismodellen tonen ernstig haarverlies, waarmee een causale rol is vastgesteld.

How hair loss happens — and where we intervene
Hoe haarverlies ontstaat — en waar we ingrijpen
1

DHT accumulation: 5α-reductase converts testosterone to DHT in the scalp. DHT binds androgen receptors in dermal papilla cells, triggering a cascade that shortens the anagen phase with each successive cycle.

DHT-accumulatie: 5α-reductase zet testosteron om in DHT in de hoofdhuid. DHT bindt androgeenreceptoren in dermale papilcellen en triggert een cascade die de anageenfase verkort.

2

Follicle miniaturisation: Over successive cycles, affected follicles produce progressively thinner, shorter hairs — eventually producing only vellus hairs or ceasing production entirely.

Follikelminiaturisatie: In opeenvolgende cycli produceren aangetaste follikels steeds dunnere, kortere haren — uiteindelijk alleen velusharen of helemaal geen productie meer.

3

Our approach: Saw palmetto inhibits 5α-reductase to reduce DHT. Biotin, L-cysteine, and zinc support keratin synthesis machinery. Vitamin D3 maintains VDR-mediated follicle cycle regulation. Together these address multiple points in the pathway.

Onze aanpak: Zaagpalm remt 5α-reductase om DHT te verminderen. Biotine, L-cysteïne en zink ondersteunen de keratinesynthesemachinery. Vitamine D3 handhaaft VDR-gemedieerde follikelcyclusregulatie.

Biotin for the treatment of nail disease, hair and skin
Biotine voor de behandeling van nagelziekten, haar en huid
Skin Appendage Disorders · Patel et al., 2017 · Systematic review, 18 reported cases

Systematic review of published case reports found biotin supplementation (2,500–5,000 mcg) was associated with improved hair quantity and quality in patients with underlying biotin deficiency or metabolic disorders affecting biotin metabolism. Evidence in biotin-sufficient individuals is limited; most studies lack placebo controls.

Systematische review van gepubliceerde casusrapporten vond dat biotinesuppletie (2.500–5.000 mcg) werd geassocieerd met verbeterde haarhoeveelheid en -kwaliteit bij patiënten met biotinedeficiëntie.

Limitation: Based on case reports rather than RCTs. Effect size in biotin-sufficient individuals remains unclear.

Beperking: Gebaseerd op casusrapporten in plaats van RCT's. Effect bij biotine-voldoende personen blijft onduidelijk.

02
Randomised trial of Serenoa repens versus placebo in mild-to-moderate androgenetic alopecia
Gerandomiseerde studie van Serenoa repens versus placebo bij androgenetische alopecia
Journal of Alternative & Complementary Medicine · Murugusundram, 2009 · RCT, n=38

Participants receiving 320 mg/day liposterolic saw palmetto extract over 24 weeks showed statistically significant improvement in hair count at the vertex (p<0.05) versus placebo. Tolerability was excellent. The authors attributed the effect to 5α-reductase inhibition reducing scalp DHT levels.

Deelnemers die 24 weken lang 320 mg/dag liposterolisch zaagpalmextract kregen, vertoonden statistisch significante verbetering in haartelling (p<0,05) versus placebo. Verdraagbaarheid was uitstekend.

Limitation: Small sample size (n=38). Single-centre. Longer-term data beyond 24 weeks not reported.

Beperking: Kleine steekproef (n=38). Eén centrum. Langetermijngegevens na 24 weken niet gerapporteerd.

03
Serum ferritin and vitamin D in female hair loss: do they play a role?
Serumferritine en vitamine D bij vrouwelijk haarverlies: spelen ze een rol?
Skin Pharmacology and Physiology · Rasheed et al., 2013 · Case-control, n=180

Serum 25(OH)D levels were significantly lower in women with telogen effluvium and androgenetic alopecia versus controls (p<0.001). The correlation persisted after adjusting for ferritin, age, and BMI, suggesting an independent association.

Serum 25(OH)D-waarden waren significant lager bij vrouwen met telogeen effluvium en androgenetische alopecia versus controles (p<0,001).

Limitation: Observational — association does not establish causation. Supplementation trials in vitamin D-sufficient populations show inconsistent results.

Beperking: Observationeel — associatie bewijst geen causaliteit. Suppletieproeven bij vitamine D-voldoende populaties tonen inconsistente resultaten.

Zinc levels in alopecia areata and androgenetic alopecia: a meta-analysis
Zinkniveaus bij alopecia areata en androgenetische alopecia: een meta-analyse
Dermatology Practical & Conceptual · Ozturk et al., 2020 · Meta-analysis, 14 studies

Pooled analysis of 14 case-control studies found significantly lower serum zinc in alopecia areata (SMD −1.05, p<0.001) and androgenetic alopecia (SMD −0.57, p=0.002) vs healthy controls. Association was consistent across populations.

Gepoolde analyse van 14 case-controlstudies vond significant lager serumzink bij alopecia areata (SMD −1,05, p<0,001) en androgenetische alopecia (SMD −0,57, p=0,002) versus gezonde controles.

Limitation: Association between deficiency and hair loss does not directly imply supplementation benefits zinc-sufficient individuals.

Beperking: Associatie tussen tekort en haarverlies impliceert niet direct dat suppletie zinkvoldoende personen ten goede komt.
